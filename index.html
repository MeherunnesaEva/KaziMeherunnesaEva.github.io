01 — About

I'm a researcher and Computer Science graduate from Daffodil International University, Dhaka. My work sits at the intersection of language, culture, and AI — specifically building models that understand code-mixed Bangla-English (Banglish) text, a language variety spoken by millions but underrepresented in NLP research.


I'm currently a Research Assistant in the DIU NLP & ML Lab, contributing to peer-reviewed publications and mentoring undergraduate students on research methodology. My interests extend into Explainable AI, secure AI systems for healthcare, and scalable deployment of ML models in real-world environments.
